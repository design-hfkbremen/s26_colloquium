# BA / MA Colloquium
We’ll meet on Wednesdays for a colloquium to discuss your process and final projects. We’re starting with inputs in the morning and will do group consultations afterwards.

Accompanying this, the _Stammtisch (Regulars’ Table)_ takes place every Thursday 5–7pm at CATE–open to anyone interested. Sharing a meal, inputs, and open discussions on graphics, design, and visual culture create an informal space for exchange, inspiration, and getting to know each other.

## Consultations
We’ll do consultations in groups of 3, every other week.
